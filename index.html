Common Questions

Will my paper be stored in Turnitin?

No. We use a "No-Repository" setting. Your paper will not be saved to Turnitin's database, so it won't show 100% similarity when you finally submit to your school.

How long does a scan take?

Most reports are generated within 3 to 10 minutes depending on the current server queue.

Is the AI detection accurate?

Our model is 99% accurate against major AI writers. However, any score below 15% should be reviewed for potential false positives.
